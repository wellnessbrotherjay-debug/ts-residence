4. May 2026 Strategy: Video-First Campaign Launch

Objective: Recover 50-60% of lost traffic through new promotional video campaign and strategic budget reallocation.

Why Video?

  • Video-driven campaigns show 3.2x higher engagement vs static content
  • Current organic traffic (71.4%) is stable - video adds paid channel recovery
  • Meta platform (FB + IG = 34.6% of current traffic) performs well with video
  • Google CPC recovery with video ad assets will target high-intent audience

May Campaign Details

Video Specifications
